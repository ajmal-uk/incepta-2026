About INCEPTA 2026

About CET

The College of Engineering, Trivandrum (CET), is a pioneering institution of technical education in Kerala. For 85 illustrious years since its foundation in 1939, CET has remained steadfast in upholding a legacy of excellence in both technical and cultural domains.

We proudly stand as a beacon of knowledge and innovation. CET consistently ranks among the foremost institutions in the country for the quality of its graduates and their significant contributions to top-tier jobs.

About ASCA

The Association of Students of Computer Applications (ASCA) is the official student body of the Department of Computer Applications at College of Engineering Trivandrum (CET). Serving as a dynamic platform for MCA students, ASCA strives to enhance academic excellence, technical competence, and professional growth.

It plays a vital role in organizing various technical and cultural initiatives that foster innovation and collaboration among students.

About INCEPTA

INCEPTA is the premier technical festival organized by the Association of Computer Applications (ASCA) of the MCA Department at the prestigious College of Engineering, Trivandrum (CET). Scheduled for January 29th and 30th, 2026, INCEPTA serves as a highly focused platform centered on the disruptive fields of AI Agents and Generative AI.

INCEPTA leverages the strong academic backing of CET and a comprehensive network across the KTU MCA student community throughout Kerala. Its core mission is to bridge the gap between academic theory and industry reality, providing sponsors with direct access to a specialized, future-ready talent pool and maximum visibility within Kerala's technical education ecosystem.
